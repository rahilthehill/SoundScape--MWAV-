Enter SoundScape

About

SoundScape is a dynamic and interactive web-based application designed to visualize real-time audio input from your microphone.

SoundScape aims to offer a appealing way to visualize your music/audio.

You can choose from four different modes: Classic, Channels, Colorful, and Smooth.

I made it with the aim to use it as a fun backdrop for your setup while you're playing music.

It's a mini project that will be updated every now and then to enhance features and performance.
